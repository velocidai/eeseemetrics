**Eesee Metrics** is a privacy-first web and product analytics platform. Get a clear picture of who visits your site, how they navigate it, and where they drop off — without cookies or GDPR headaches.

## Getting Started

Sign up for the [cloud version](https://app.eeseemetrics.com/signup) and get up and running in minutes.

Then learn how to add the [tracking script](/docs/script) to your website to start collecting data.
