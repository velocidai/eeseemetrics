Eesee Metrics automatically monitors your site and delivers insights without requiring you to open a dashboard. Reports are generated on a schedule, anomalies are detected daily, and your analytics data is accessible to any MCP-compatible AI tool.

## What's included

<Cards>
  <Card title="AI Reports" href="/docs/piel/ai-reports">
    Structured plain-English analytics reports generated on a schedule — weekly, monthly, quarterly, and yearly. Stored in your dashboard and delivered by email.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Weekly reports" href="/docs/piel/weekly-reports">
    Every Monday, a plain-English email summary of the past week lands in your inbox — top pages, top referrers, biggest changes versus the prior week.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Anomaly alerts" href="/docs/piel/anomaly-alerts">
    Your key metrics are monitored daily. When something unusual happens — a traffic spike, a drop in sessions, a sudden bounce rate change — you're notified automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="MCP integration" href="/docs/api/mcp">
    Connect your analytics data to Claude, Cursor, or any AI tool that supports the Model Context Protocol. Ask questions about your site in plain English.
  </Card>
</Cards>

## Who it's for

- **Founders and solo operators** — get a Monday-morning briefing in your inbox instead of digging through charts
- **Marketers** — know when a campaign is working (or breaking) before you check manually
- **Engineers** — connect your analytics to Claude or Cursor and query your data while you work
- **Teams** — every member in your organization gets the same weekly summary automatically

## Getting started

Weekly reports and anomaly alerts are enabled by default for Pro and Scale accounts. No configuration required — they start running automatically on your data from the moment you upgrade.

To manage your preferences:

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications** in your dashboard
2. Toggle weekly email reports on or off per site
3. Configure alert channels (Slack, Discord, webhook, email) per site under **Site Settings → Alerts**
