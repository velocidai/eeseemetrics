title: AI Reports

Eesee Metrics automatically generates plain-English analytics reports on a schedule based on your plan. Reports are stored in your dashboard so you can browse, read, and share them at any time — and retrieve them via the MCP integration.

## What's in a report

Each report is a structured AI-generated summary covering:

- **Overview metrics** — sessions, pageviews, unique visitors, bounce rate, and average session duration for the period, with percentage changes versus the prior equivalent period
- **Visitor split** — new vs. returning visitor ratio
- **Summary** — a 2–4 sentence plain-English narrative of the period's performance
- **Highlights** — key findings tagged as positive, negative, or neutral, with the metric and observation
- **Goals** — conversion counts and conversion rates for your configured goals
- **Top pages** — the 5 pages that drove the most sessions
- **Top referrers** — top traffic sources
- **Top countries** — geographic breakdown
- **Device breakdown** — desktop / mobile / tablet split
- **Recommendations** — 3–5 actionable suggestions based on the period's data
- **Top search queries** — if Google Search Console is connected, the top queries by clicks with position data

## Report cadence

| Cadence | Period covered | When generated | Plan |
|---|---|---|---|
| Weekly | Past 7 days | Every Monday | Pro, Scale |
| Monthly | Past calendar month | 1st of each month | Scale |
| Quarterly | Past calendar quarter | 1st of each quarter | Scale |
| Yearly | Past calendar year | January 1st | Scale |

Reports are generated automatically — no action required. If a site has fewer than 7 days of data when a weekly report is due, that report is skipped until there's enough history.

## Accessing reports

1. Open your site in the dashboard
2. Go to **Insights → Reports** in the sidebar
3. Browse the list — each row shows the cadence, the period covered, and the generation time
4. Click any report to open the full structured view

Reports are sorted by date, most recent first. A small badge on the sidebar item shows how many unread reports are waiting.

## Email delivery

Reports are also delivered by email. The weekly report arrives every Monday. Monthly, quarterly, and yearly reports are emailed when generated.

To toggle email delivery:

1. Go to **Insights → Reports**
2. Use the **Email reports** toggle in the top-right corner of the page

This setting is per-account and only affects your email — other organization members are not affected.

## Retrieving reports via MCP

If you have the MCP integration connected, you can retrieve reports directly from Claude or Cursor:

```
get_latest_report()                    — most recent report of any cadence
get_latest_report(cadence: "monthly")  — most recent monthly report
get_all_reports()                      — list of all available reports (Scale)
```

The AI tool returns the full structured summary, which you can then ask follow-up questions about.
