---
title: Weekly Reports
description: Automatic Monday morning analytics summaries delivered to your inbox
---

Every Monday at midnight UTC, Eesee generates a plain-English email summary of the past 7 days for every site in your organization. No dashboards, no manual work.

## What's in the report

Each weekly report covers:

- **Overview metrics** — total sessions, pageviews, unique visitors, bounce rate, and average session duration for the past 7 days
- **Week-over-week comparison** — how each metric changed versus the previous 7-day period, with percentage deltas
- **Top pages** — the 5 pages that drove the most sessions
- **Top referrers** — the 5 sources that sent the most traffic
- **Top countries** — where your visitors came from
- **Device breakdown** — desktop vs. mobile vs. tablet split

If you have multiple sites in your organization, each site gets its own section in the email.

## Delivery

- **Schedule**: Every Monday at 00:00 UTC
- **Recipient**: Every member of your organization (who has access to the site)
- **Format**: HTML email via Resend

Reports respect your organization's member permissions — if a team member only has access to certain sites, they only receive summaries for those sites.

## Opting out

Weekly reports are on by default. To disable them for your account:

1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**
2. Toggle **Weekly email reports** off

This only affects your account. Other organization members keep receiving their reports.

## Accessing past reports in the dashboard

Your reports are also stored and accessible directly in the dashboard:

1. Open your site
2. Go to **Insights → Reports**
3. Browse or search past weekly, monthly, quarterly, and yearly reports

You can also retrieve reports via the MCP integration using `get_latest_report` or `get_all_reports`.
